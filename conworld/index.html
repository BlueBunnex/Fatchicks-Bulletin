Bastite, a blue metal that is dropped by those pesky goblins in the mountains. It isn't magical, nor is it particularly tough, but it's lightweight and it's abundant, which is good enough for most adventurers' gear.

Bastite-tin alloy is used for roofing, superior to tin alone by being ever-so-slightly more malleable (and cheaper, too).
