Pokémon Fixation

I'm a Pokémon conservative that only acknowledges Pokémon introduced in Red/Blue as canon. As in, if I were to create a perfect Pokémon world, it would only have the original 151 (who needs lopunny or gardevoir when you got Serena am I right).

Outside of that however, I really like the designs in Pokémon games and the Pokémon world in general. I think Pokémon is a pretty fun game (at least the old ones whose ROMs you can play on your phone), with half the draw from the world-building, half from the open-ended gameplay, and half from the lustability of the characters.

On the topic of worldbuilding, I'm a big fan of the "hobbyist-trainer" dichotomy that sometimes appears in dialogues. I'd be a hobbyist of course, it sounds more chill.

On the topic of characters, my favourite is Rosa, but only by a smidge (the OG Leaf is fairly cute too). I wonder if there are any good trinkets I can get of her...
