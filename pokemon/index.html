Pokémon Fixation

See, I think Pokémon is actually a pretty fun game (at least the old ones whose ROMs you can play on your phone), but I'm also here for the booba. My favourite character from Pokémon is Rosa, but only by a smidge. I wonder if there are any good trinkets I can get of her...

At the end of the day these little critters are pretty cute.
