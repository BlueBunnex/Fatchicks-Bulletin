PokéWeekly

For me, Pokémon's draw comes from three places: World-building, open-ended gameplay, and appealing character designs (and their resulting lust potential).

In my perfect Pokémon world, I would invite only pokémon introduced in Red/Blue. As for trainers, only the cute ones.

I hesitate to say that my favourite character in the series is Rosa because it's only by a smidge. Their design is super appealing, but many others are too!

I'm a big fan of the "hobbyist-trainer" dichotomy that sometimes appears in dialogues. I'd be a hobbyist of course, it sounds more chill.
