en tant que

en tant que is similar to comme, and often both are translated as "as". However, there's a difference:

en tant que /ɑ̃ tɑ̃ kə/, en qualité /ka.li.te/ que -- in the capacity that (of), inasmuch that (of), insofar that (of)

En tant que vampire j'aime le sang. -- As much as a vampire, I love blood.
