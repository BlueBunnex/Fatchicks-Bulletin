「My Mentor Is So Demanding!」

Title is tentative

Note: This comic series will not be good, but it will be mine and will make good practice for writing and drawing a story.

  • persist and follow through
  • have a SMALL premise and have a PLAN; easier to stick to (structuring also helps because once you understand that, you can make pretty much any story you want since they all follow the same outlines)
  • enjoy BOTH drawing and writing
  • Storyboard the composition (with text) instead of immediately going at it

Synopsis

Fantasy world, small mountain village. Two prominent characters, one is the mentor of the other, an apprentice in magic. The mentor is charming, but unreasonably demanding and persistent, often to the annoyance of the apprentice. Other characters also exist, like townspeople.

Arc 1

Apprentice needs to prove to her mentor that she is hardworking before learning her first spell. Thus, her mentor puts her through trials, cooking meals, cleaning, doing yardwork, and running errands in the village.

Shot of outside of building with text "three more months?!"; Then inside with apprentice and mentor, mentor saying "Yes. I believe it will take that long for you to become ready for magic."

Apprentice yelling at mentor with a flashback panel of a messageboard, "I thought your advert said you provided quick results??"; Shot of mentor gesturing towards apprentice's belly, "That was a lie." "At least the magic part, you have been eating my food rather quickly."
