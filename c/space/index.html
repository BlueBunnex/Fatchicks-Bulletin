Selorians

Selorians are a developed reptilian alien species. They are classified as COOPERATIVE by the United Stellar Family (USF). Of biological interest are their extremely large, breast-like organs that inflate with air as a preemptive, unconscious response to an anticipated reduction in air quality or availability. Most Selorian life support units do not include an oxygen recycler as a result.
