Medicine ball is love. Medicine ball is life.

I am getting back into fitness. At my gym we have medicine balls that I can use to exercises like:

  • Pickups (starting in a squat with the ball on the ground in front of you, then standing straight while lifting the ball above your head)
  • Around the Worlds (holding the ball above your head, then slowly and controlledly swinging it to the side and behind your head)
  • Up and Outs (holding the ball straight up, bringing it to your chest, then pushing it straight out)

Medicine balls are good for training strength and coordination.
