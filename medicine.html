I am getting back into fitness. At my gym we have medicine balls that I can use for exercises like:

  • Pickups
    1. Start in a squat with the ball on the ground in front of you.
    2. Raise the ball while standing up straight so that it ends up above your head.
    3. Squat back down while lowering the ball to its starting position.
  • Around the Worlds (holding the ball above your head, then slowly and controlledly swinging it to the side and behind your head)
  • Up and Outs (holding the ball straight up, bringing it to your chest, then pushing it straight out)

Medicine balls are good for training strength and coordination. I think doing them after your main exercise will help squeeze a little extra muscle out of you!
