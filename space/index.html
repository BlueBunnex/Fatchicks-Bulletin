I don't like the IAU's definition of a planet, so I made my own.

  1. Is in orbit around a sun,
  2. has sufficient mass to assume hydrostatic equilibrium (a nearly round shape).

A natural celestial body that satisfies both criteria is a planet. If it does not satisfy point 1, add "rogue" to the beginning. If it does not satisfy point 2, replace planet with "planetoid."
